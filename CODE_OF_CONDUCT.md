# Code Of Conduct

This project welcomes constructive participation from learners, users, and maintainers.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent when asking or answering questions.
- Keep issues focused on reproducible facts, environment details, and actionable next steps.
- Credit prior work and community answers when reusing them.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or repeated duplicate requests.
- Demanding private support outside the repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or close comments and issues that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocking from the repository.
